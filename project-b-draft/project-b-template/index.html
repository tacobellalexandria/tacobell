Easter Island Enigma: an interactive adventure

Where is Easter Island?

- Easter Island, or Rapa Nui is located off the coast of South America's pacific southern cone region.


Who owns the island?

- The island is officially governed by Chile! Residents speak Spanish and Rapa Nui.


What are the iconic statues on Easter Island called?

- They're called Moai, in the indigenous Rapa Nui language.

Fun fact #1

- There are nearly 900 Moai on the island.


Fun fact #2

- The Moai surround the coasts of the island, with most of them facing outland.


Fun fact #3

- The Moai have bodies, estimated to be around 3 to 11 meters long and averaging 82 tonnes.
